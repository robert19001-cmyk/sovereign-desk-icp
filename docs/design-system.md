# Design System

SovereignDesk uses a premium white/gold SaaS direction.

## Design Thesis

The public surface should feel like a diligence packet, not a crypto demo. Gold is used as proof and hierarchy, while dark ink carries text for contrast and readability.

## Tokens

- Background: warm white / ivory.
- Primary text: dark charcoal.
- Accent: restrained gold and champagne.
- Status: green for live, gold for waiting, neutral for quiet.
- Radius: compact 6-8px.
- Motion: subtle reveal and hover only, with `prefers-reduced-motion` support.

## Signature Move

The live proof ledger uses a gold vault graphic and tabbed product modules:

- Reviewer
- Operator
- Client
- Trust

## Rules

- Do not use pale gold for body text on white.
- Use gold mostly for borders, active tabs, proof accents, and focused hierarchy.
- Keep ICP proof visible, but keep the product promise first.
- Keep canister IDs, principals, hashes, notes, and commands wrapped.
- Every major section must remain usable at `320px`, `390px`, `768px`, `1024px`, and `1440px`.
